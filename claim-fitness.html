ABOUT CLAIM FITNESS

Your path to better health and fitness starts here!

Welcome to Claim Fitness, located in the heart of Yorba Linda, CA. At Claim Fitness, we offer a range of specialized fitness programs including Boot Camp, Strength & Conditioning, and Cardio Boxing designed to help you achieve your personal fitness goals. Whether you're looking to lose weight, build muscle, or improve athletic performance, our expert coaches are here to guide you every step of the way.

Meet Coach Carlos M.


Coach Carlos M., the Owner and Fitness Director of Claim Fitness, is an NPTA-certified Master Trainer, USATF-certified Elite Coach, and has a B.S. in Kinesiology from Texas A&M Kingsville. With over 20 years of experience, including training elite athletes and everyday individuals, Coach Carlos has developed methods that have helped 64 scholarship athletes and 10 professional athletes reach their goals. His approach focuses on athleticism and holistic health.
